3D moddeling

File created by Brandon Winter.

First Test:

Using autodesk fusion 360, this was the first model of a gear made and rendered. This gear was put together using a series of steps and tools provided in autodesk and exported into an SLI file to be viewed on the website. The steps included drawing a sketch of the object required following a reference, alongside extruding the sketch into a 3d shape, and picking a background and adjusting the rotation to view the gear

Updated/Improved:

This is a follow up of the previous model that had proper materials applied being polished brass as well as having the edges inside smooth using "fillet" tools, this provided a much cleaner looking finish and much better results with rendering.

Extra Changes:

To get a more indepth visualization of the rendering capabilities of the 3d modelling software, I used a sphere to emit light which was placed above the gear. Editting the apperance and meterials allowed me to change the colours and brightness. This paired with changing the background colour and lighting effects provided much better shadows and clearer visuals of the gear.
